How to Migrate Your Wordle History to e65ce0a5-wordle

  1. First, create a Wordle Migration Tool by dragging this to your browser’s bookmark bar.
  2. Secondly, download your Wordle history to file by first navigating to Wordle and then clicking the previously created Wordle Migration Tool on your browser’s bookmark bar.
  3. Finally, upload the previously saved Wordle history file to e65ce0a5-wordle by first navigating to e65ce0a5-wordle and then clicking the Wordle Migration Tool on your browser’s bookmark bar for a second time.
Note that your Wordle history is held in your browser’s local storage and will be lost any time the storage is cleared, either manually or automatically when a private/incognito browser session is ended. Use the Wordle Migration Tool to restore a previously saved history if your browser’s local storage is cleared. You can download your Wordle history as often as you like, but you can only restore it when your browser’s local storage has been cleared.
Full Disclosure: Unfortunately, unlike me, Wordle “in-the-wild” doesn’t record which puzzles you’ve solved beyond your last win and the length of your current winning streak. So when you migrate your “in-the-wild” history to me, any puzzles beyond your last win and current streak are taken to be unsolved and are available to be replayed.
