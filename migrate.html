How to Migrate Your Wordle History to e65ce0a5-wordle

  1. First, create a Wordle Migration Tool by dragging this to your bookmark bar.
  2. Secondly, save your Wordle history to file by first navigating to Wordle then clicking the previously created Wordle Migration Tool on your bookmark bar.
  3. Finally, upload your previously saved Wordle history file to e65ce0a5-wordle by first navigating to e65ce0a5-wordle then clicking the Wordle Migration Tool on your bookmark bar.
  • Note that your Wordle history is held in your browser’s local storage and will be lost if your browser’s local storage is cleared, either manually or automatically when a private/incognito brower session is ended. Use the Wordle Migration Tool to restore a previously saved history if your browser’s local storage is cleared.
